About The Boba Teashop

The Boba Teashop is a first-person horror game. You take on a part-time job at a small bubble tea shop at the end of an alley. At first, everything is peaceful: making drinks, serving customers, and cleaning. But after a few nights, you start to realize that things are not normal.

How to play The Boba Teashop

Complete shifts at the bubble tea shop, fulfill orders, keep your cool, and uncover the terrifying secret hidden under the shop's sweet facade.

Each night, you start your shift at the counter. Read orders from customers and make them correctly.

Make the right drinks, but don't trust the recipe too much.

Some orders have strange ingredients: "red pearls," "brown sugar water from the back alley," "frozen milk from a previous customer"

You can follow them or try to change them to see what happens.

You may notice unpleasant details or small changes in the shop after each shift.

Reflections that don't look like you. Shadows behind doors. Bills that don't match reality.

Gradually, you'll unlock hidden rooms, old tape recorders, diaries, and even… evidence of crimes.

Some items must be used at the right time if you don't want to "alarm someone."

While serving customers, you have to keep the shop running smoothly to avoid being "fired" or worse.

Missing too many orders, reacting incorrectly, or being negligent will lead to unwanted consequences.

Have fun playing The Boba Teashop and escape safely!
